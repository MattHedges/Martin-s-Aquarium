Diet

The piranha isn't very picky but it can devour almost anything. It mainly only goes after dead prey. A whole school of piranhas can eat an entire chicken in a matter of minutes.

Environment

The piranha is a fresh water fish. It needs to be kept in temperatures between 75 to 80 degrees Fahrenheit

Habitat

Piranhas can live alongside other fish but carnivourous piranahas will eat or nibble at smaller ones.

Safety

NEVER stick your hand in a Piranha enclosure. Whereas typically the Piranha won't attempt to bite live or larger prey, it is still not recommended to test out this theory. Play stupid games, win stupid prizes.

Tank Care

Clean weekly due to leftover residue from food. Keep water between 75-80 degrees Fahrenheit
